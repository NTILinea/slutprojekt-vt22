Stegen is a simple dice game where you need to roll all the numbers through 1 to 6 in order with a single dice. It dosen't need to be in perfect order. for excample: if you roll 5, 3, 1, 4, 3, 2 in that order, you'll still get 1 and 2 and can just contine form there till you've reached 6. the goal is to roll all the numbers in order in lest amount of rolls. Have fun!
